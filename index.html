About

Claude-Mini is a teaching distillation of claude-code-my-workflow. It bundles a starter kit of 16 skills, 10 agents, 10 rules, 2 hooks — adapted for general academic use — alongside a 4-hour walk-through and 6 pre-staged demo bundles.

Audience: academic researchers in applied empirical fields. Format: watch-along — the speaker demonstrates live; the audience observes. No participant install required to follow.

This repo is a snapshot, not a maintained mirror. Bug fixes and new skills land in the parent template first. Treat Claude-Mini as a launching pad; fork the parent for active work.
